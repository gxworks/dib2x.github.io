Dib2x ('dibdib') Family of Apps

Dib2Calc, aka Dibdib Calculator: https://gitlab.com/dibdib/Dib2Calc

Dib2Qm, aka Dibdib Messenger: https://gitlab.com/gxworks/Dib2Qm

Dib2J: https://gitlab.com/dibdib/Dib2J

Dib2Co, aka Dibdib Console (reader): https://gitlab.com/gxworks/Dib2Co

Each app's full project is stored under gitlab.com (as noted), the shared files are stored under dibdib.sf.net, the overview is stored under dib2x.github.io.
